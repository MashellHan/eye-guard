## Architecture Quality

- **Protocol-oriented**: All major components behind protocols for testability
- **Immutable patterns**: Models use structs, break events use copy-on-write
- **Strict concurrency**: Swift 6 with @MainActor, Sendable, @Observable
- **Separation of concerns**: 15+ focused modules, each under 400 lines
- **Dependency injection**: BreakScheduler accepts injectable ActivityMonitoring and NotificationSending

## Known Issues

1. `CGWindowListCreateImage` deprecation warning (macOS 14+) — should migrate to ScreenCaptureKit
2. `swift-testing` package dependency shows deprecation warnings (bundled in Swift 6 toolchain)
3. Night mode uses hardcoded 10 PM - 6 AM (should be configurable in preferences)
